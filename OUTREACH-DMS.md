## Usage Notes

- **Personalise heavily** — fill in [brackets] before sending. Generic DMs get ignored.
- **Timing:** Tuesday–Thursday, 10am–2pm typically gets the best response rates.
- **Follow-up:** If no reply in 5 days, send a single follow-up with a different angle (e.g. a specific result or a question).
- **Volume cap:** Don't spam. Target 10–20 DMs per day max, high quality over quantity.
- **Platform tips:**
  - Instagram: Keep it short. Leads with the first line — make it count.
  - LinkedIn: Slightly more formal tone. Use Template 2 or 3.
  - Email: All three work. Add a subject line and sign off with full name + title.
